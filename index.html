About Me

There’s an old Chinese proverb that says, the best time to plant a tree was 20 years ago. The second best time is now. When my partner and I started a CD and DVD duplication business in 1997, record labels and independent artists were looking for an alternative to the cumbersome process of manufacturing. Hard work and a high value for customer satisfaction helped us make the most of that opportunity. Now I‘m excited about planting a new tree and expanding my comfort zone. Pursuing a career in Web Development is both a return to my roots in programming and a step into dynamic learning. I enjoy the craftsmanship of the process, the combination of creativity, design, and analysis. The biggest draw is the opportunity it affords to be part of a team that shepherds a vision to reality and creates something bigger than the sum of its parts.
